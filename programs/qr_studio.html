Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to type my WiFi Password here?

Yes. This tool runs Client-Side. Your WiFi password is converted into the QR pattern directly in your browser. It is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

What is SVG format?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) allows you to resize the QR code to any size (even billboard size) without it becoming blurry/pixelated. Perfect for print designers.

Can I change the link later?

No. Since this is a Static QR Code, the data is hard-coded into the image. If you change your website URL or WiFi password, you must generate a new QR code.
